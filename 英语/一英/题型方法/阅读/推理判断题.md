# 推理判断
**WHY**推理判断题最难?
## 浅分个类
- 推理判断题
    - 高级细节题 infer/learn imply/suggest
    - 隐形主旨题
- 观点态度题
    - 积极的, 支持的, 赞同的
    - 消极的, 否定的, 批评的
    - 客观的
- 写作目的题
- 文章出处题
## 推断 or 细节
有些题有 infer/learn imply/suggest 等关键词,但是是细节理解题
|Visiting an apple event is a good chance to see, and often taste, a wide variety of apples. To people who are used to the limited choice of apples such as Golden Delicious and Royal Gala in supermarkets, it can be quite an eye opener to see the range of classical apples still in existence, such as Decio which was grown by the Romans. Although it doesn't taste of anything special, it is still worth a try, as is the knobbly(疙瘩的) Cat's Head which is more if a curiosity than anything else.|
|---|
|What can we learn about Decio?|
|`A. It is a new variety.`|
|`B. It has a strange look.`|
|`C. It is rarely seen now.`|
|`D. It has a special taste.`|
|`the range of classical apples` 可知 Decio 不是一个新品种|
|B很容易选,因为 `it can be quite an eye opener` 但是 `it` 指代的是后面的 `to see the range of classical apples still in existence` 所以B是主观臆断|
|D `Although it doesn't taste of anything special` 所以D是错误的|
